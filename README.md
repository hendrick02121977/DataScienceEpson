# Data Science Course

## Course Overview
Welcome to the Data Science course designed for aspiring data scientists looking to enhance their skills in data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling. This course will guide you through the fundamental concepts and techniques used in the field of data science.

## Prerequisites
Before starting this course, you should have:
- Basic knowledge of programming in Python.
- A fundamental understanding of statistics and mathematics.
- Familiarity with data manipulation libraries such as Pandas and NumPy is a plus.

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- Understand the key concepts in data science.
- Perform data analysis using Python.
- Build and evaluate machine learning models.
- Visualize data to communicate findings effectively.
- Utilize Google Colab for collaborative coding and sharing your work.

## Instructions to Open Each Day's Notebook in Google Colab
To open each day's notebook, follow these steps:
1. Click on the link provided for each day's session in the course schedule.
2. This will redirect you to Google Colab (you may need to log in with your Google account).
3. Once in Google Colab, click on 'Open in Playground' or 'Copy to Drive' to make the notebook accessible for your edits.

Feel free to explore the notebooks at your own pace, and don't hesitate to reach out for help as needed.
